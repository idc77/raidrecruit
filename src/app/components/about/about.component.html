About

contested.de is a free to use site built out of necessity.

The problem it aims to address is if you play Everquest 2, which is a MMORPG, especially if you play on TLE, you like to group up with people. A Raid is a larger group. In fact it can be up to 4 groups in size. However when you have up to 24 people playing together at the same time, needs arise. Needs for management of that raid, loot distribution, advertising, organisation. For that cause traditionally guilds took over the role of those kinds of things.

However when you join a raid guild you usually have to be available at certain times on certain days. Even if you don't feel like you'd like to raid today.

This is where this site, contested.de, comes into place.

This site allows you to create raids and sign up for raids.

Contested Guild

There is also a "contested" guild located on the servers "Fallen Gate" and "Thurgadin". Any friendly, mature person is invited to join.
This site is of course also an attempt to find cool people to play, hang out and raid with.
"Contested" guild's main timezones are Europe and Australia.

FAQ

Is this thing secure?
The account portion is as secure as it can be at the current state of technology. The webapp however wasn't written with security and tampering protection in mind. Someone who knows what he's doing will not have a very hard time for instance acting as someone he is not or joining raids he's not supposed to. I'm not going to actively persue an offender. However, should a person repeatedly try to harm this service and its users I won't back down to report this behaviour to the authorities. All actions are logged. I built this service to be useful and enjoyable for everyone and kept it as simple as possible. So there's no glory in absuing it.
